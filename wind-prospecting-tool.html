Surface Roughness
Roughness length (z0) describes surface friction. Lower z0 = smoother terrain = faster wind. Typical: Water 0.0002m, Grass 0.03m, Farmland 0.05m, Forest 0.5-1.0m, Urban 1.0-2.0m
