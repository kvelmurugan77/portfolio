Procurement Project Setup

Configure the wind project procurement context. The tool captures the full EPC procurement lifecycle — from capex planning and strategic sourcing through vendor management, techno-commercial negotiation, contract execution, and capex/cash-flow tracking — across all major scope categories: WTG, BoP (Civil & Electrical), Transformers, Cables, Switchyard, SCADA.

Tool Capabilities — Maps to Job Requirements
Wind EPC / Project Procurement
  • ▸ End-to-end EPC procurement workflow — BoQ, RFQ, contracts, execution, closeout
  • ▸ Project procurement plan with long-lead item identification & critical path
  • ▸ Scope category coverage: WTG, BoP Civil, BoP Electrical, Transformers, Cables, Switchyard, SCADA
Vendor Management & Strategic Sourcing
  • ▸ Approved Vendor List (AVL) with pre-qualification tracking & expiries
  • ▸ 5-axis vendor evaluation: Technical, Commercial, HSE, Financial, Delivery
  • ▸ Strategic sourcing — category strategy, market intelligence, single/dual/multi-source policy
  • ▸ Vendor performance scorecards with post-delivery rating & lessons learnt
Techno-Commercial Negotiation
  • ▸ RFQ register with multi-vendor issuance & bid validity tracking
  • ▸ Bid comparison matrix — technical compliance + commercial scoring
  • ▸ Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) calculator — Capex + Opex + Spares + LD exposure
  • ▸ Should-cost analysis & negotiation tracker with savings capture
Capex Procurement & Contract Management
  • ▸ Contract register with milestone tracking, LD calculation, variations & warranty
  • ▸ Cash-flow S-curve — planned vs actual capex deployment with variance
  • ▸ Payment milestone tracker with retention, advance, & performance bond
  • ▸ Expediting & delivery tracking — manufacturing, shipment, site receipt
