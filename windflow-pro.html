Roughness Map
Why roughness matters: Surface roughness (z₀) determines wind shear — taller obstacles = slower near-surface wind = higher shear. Different directions have different terrain (forest vs sea vs open fields), so per-sector roughness is needed for accurate WAsP-style OWC→GWC→SWC wind atlas chain.
Tries ESA CCI first, falls back to OSM Enhanced · Auto-generates roughness zones + 16-sector directional rose
