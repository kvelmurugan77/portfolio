⚠
Screening, not a yaw correction. SCADA direction channels can be biased by wind-vane offset, turbine wake, terrain, curtailment, anemometer error and control logic. This tool prioritises a turbine for nacelle-mounted LiDAR confirmation; it must not be used alone to change a yaw offset or guarantee an AEP gain.

LiDAR decision note
    Method and interpretation guardrails
    1. Direction-offset signal
    Nacelle direction minus SCADA wind direction is analysed circularly during filtered, partial-load operation. It is a direction-channel diagnostic, not automatically a physical rotor-to-inflow yaw angle.
    2. Power-response signal
    Power is normalised within turbine / wind-speed bins and assessed against direction offset. Its purpose is corroboration only; it may be weak where yaw control holds one narrow offset range.
    3. Priority, not proof
    Priority combines magnitude, agreement, sample volume and repeatability, then discounts data issues. Confirm direction conventions, status codes, curtailment logic and a nacelle-LiDAR plan before intervention.
